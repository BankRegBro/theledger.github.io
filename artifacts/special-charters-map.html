This map documents states and territories that have enacted specialized financial charters and licenses — frameworks outside the standard insured depository model that reflect the frontier of American financial regulation.
Special Charter — State authority to operate as a specialized depository or financial institution, typically without FDIC insurance or with narrowly scoped banking powers.
Special License — A regulatory license enabling virtual currency or other digital financial asset business activity, distinct from a full bank or trust charter.
Charter + License — Jurisdictions with both a notable specialized charter and an innovative financial services license. Currently California and New York.
